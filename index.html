About Me

I’m a PhD student at Zhejiang University under the supervision of Prof. Jun Xiao（Sep 2020 - present). I’m also currently at Singapore Management University as a visiting PhD, under the supervision of Prof. Qianru Sun (Sep 2022 - present). I got my B.E. degree in Ocean engineering at Zhejiang University (Sep 2016 - Jun 2020).

My research interests mainly include computer vision and scene understanding.
